Peanut Butter and Jelly Sandwich

A Peanut Butter and Jelly Sandwich is a simple yet delicious dish.
I love them because they're very easy to make and require almost no actual cooking skill.

Ingredients :

  • 250g of Peanut Butter
  • 250g of Jelly
  • 2 slices of White Bread

Directions:

  1. Take the two slices of bread 🍞
  2. Spread the peanut butter on one piece of bread 🥜🧈
  3. Spread the jelly on the other side.🍓
  4. Your Sandwich should look something like this
  5. Put the two pieces of bread together to form a sandwich 🥪
  6. The End Product shoudl ideally look like this
  7. Toddler 👶 adaptation: cut off crusts before serving
